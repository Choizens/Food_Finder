Recipe Finder

In my experience while going through college away from home and not being much of a cook at the time(I wasn't a vivid watcher of the Ramsay in 10 series on YouTube), I found myself always with a lot of loose ingredients that I didn't know what to do with. Recipe Finder is an app created with that main focus, where the user can enter a bunch of ingredients in a search bar, and get a list of recipes that match those parameters and some variations.
