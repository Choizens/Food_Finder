Calderetang Manok

45 minutes
4 servings
Medium

Ingredients

  • ✓ 1 kg chicken, cut into serving pieces
  • ✓ 1 cup tomato sauce
  • ✓ 1/2 cup liver spread
  • ✓ 2 medium potatoes, cubed
  • ✓ 1 large carrot, cubed
  • ✓ 1 red bell pepper, cut into strips
  • ✓ 1 green bell pepper, cut into strips
  • ✓ 1 medium onion, chopped
  • ✓ 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • ✓ 2 bay leaves
  • ✓ 1 cup chicken broth
  • ✓ 1/4 cup cooking oil
  • ✓ Salt and pepper to taste
  • ✓ 1/2 cup green olives (optional)

Instructions

  1. 1 Heat oil in a pan and sauté garlic and onion until fragrant.
  2. 2 Add chicken pieces and cook until lightly browned.
  3. 3 Pour in tomato sauce and liver spread. Mix well.
  4. 4 Add chicken broth and bay leaves. Bring to a boil.
  5. 5 Simmer for 20-25 minutes or until chicken is tender.
  6. 6 Add potatoes and carrots. Cook until vegetables are tender.
  7. 7 Add bell peppers and olives. Cook for 3-5 minutes.
  8. 8 Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  9. 9 Serve hot with steamed rice.

Sisig Paella

60 minutes
6 servings
Hard

Ingredients

  • ✓ 2 cups paella rice (or short-grain rice)
  • ✓ 500g pork belly, diced
  • ✓ 200g chicken liver, chopped
  • ✓ 1 large onion, chopped
  • ✓ 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • ✓ 3 cups chicken stock
  • ✓ 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • ✓ 3 tablespoons calamansi juice (or lemon)
  • ✓ 2-3 red chili peppers, chopped
  • ✓ 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • ✓ 1 green bell pepper, diced
  • ✓ Salt and pepper to taste
  • ✓ 2 eggs
  • ✓ Spring onions for garnish

Instructions

  1. 1 Boil pork belly until tender, then grill until crispy. Dice into small pieces.
  2. 2 In a paella pan, sauté garlic and onion until fragrant.
  3. 3 Add pork sisig and chicken liver. Cook until liver is done.
  4. 4 Add rice and stir to coat with oil and flavors.
  5. 5 Pour in chicken stock, soy sauce, and calamansi juice.
  6. 6 Add chili peppers, bell peppers. Mix well.
  7. 7 Let it simmer without stirring until rice absorbs the liquid.
  8. 8 Lower heat, cover, and cook until rice is tender (15-20 minutes).
  9. 9 Create wells in the rice and crack eggs into them.
  10. 10 Cover and cook until eggs are set.
  11. 11 Garnish with spring onions and serve hot.

Kung Pao Chicken

30 minutes
4 servings
Easy

Ingredients

  • ✓ 500g chicken breast, cubed
  • ✓ 1/2 cup roasted peanuts
  • ✓ 8-10 dried red chilies
  • ✓ 1 bell pepper, cubed
  • ✓ 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • ✓ 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • ✓ 3 green onions, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • ✓ 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • ✓ 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • ✓ 1 tablespoon Chinese rice wine (or dry sherry)
  • ✓ 1 tablespoon sugar
  • ✓ 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • ✓ 2 teaspoons sesame oil
  • ✓ 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • ✓ Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. 1 Marinate chicken with 1 tbsp soy sauce, rice wine, and cornstarch for 15 minutes.
  2. 2 Mix sauce: remaining soy sauce, vinegar, sugar, sesame oil, and 2 tbsp water.
  3. 3 Heat oil in a wok over high heat.
  4. 4 Add dried chilies and stir-fry for 30 seconds until fragrant.
  5. 5 Add chicken and stir-fry until cooked through (5-6 minutes).
  6. 6 Add garlic and ginger, stir-fry for 30 seconds.
  7. 7 Add bell pepper and stir-fry for 2 minutes.
  8. 8 Pour in the sauce and mix well.
  9. 9 Add peanuts and green onions. Toss everything together.
  10. 10 Cook for 1-2 minutes until sauce thickens.
  11. 11 Serve hot with steamed rice.

Pork Adobo

50 minutes
5 servings
Easy

Ingredients

  • ✓ 1 kg pork belly, cut into chunks
  • ✓ 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • ✓ 1/3 cup white vinegar
  • ✓ 6 cloves garlic, crushed
  • ✓ 3 bay leaves
  • ✓ 1 teaspoon whole black peppercorns
  • ✓ 1 cup water
  • ✓ 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • ✓ 1 tablespoon sugar (optional)
  • ✓ Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. 1 Combine pork, soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, bay leaves, and peppercorns in a bowl.
  2. 2 Marinate for at least 30 minutes (overnight for best results).
  3. 3 Transfer everything to a pot and add water.
  4. 4 Bring to a boil, then lower heat and simmer for 40-50 minutes until pork is tender.
  5. 5 Remove pork from the sauce and set aside.
  6. 6 Heat oil in a pan and fry pork pieces until browned on all sides.
  7. 7 Pour the sauce back into the pan with the pork.
  8. 8 Add sugar if desired. Simmer until sauce reduces and thickens.
  9. 9 Adjust seasoning with salt if needed.
  10. 10 Serve hot with steamed rice.

Everything Salmon Sheet Pan Dinner

25 minutes
2 servings
Easy

Ingredients

  • ✓ 4 salmon fillets (6 oz each)
  • ✓ 2 cups broccoli florets
  • ✓ 2 cups baby potatoes, halved
  • ✓ 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • ✓ 1 yellow bell pepper, sliced
  • ✓ 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • ✓ 2 tablespoons everything bagel seasoning
  • ✓ 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • ✓ 1 lemon, sliced
  • ✓ Salt and pepper to taste
  • ✓ Fresh dill for garnish

Instructions

  1. 1 Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. 2 Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper.
  3. 3 Toss potatoes with 1 tbsp olive oil, salt, and pepper. Arrange on one side of the sheet.
  4. 4 Roast potatoes for 15 minutes.
  5. 5 Remove sheet from oven. Add broccoli and bell peppers. Drizzle with 1 tbsp olive oil.
  6. 6 Place salmon fillets on the sheet. Brush with remaining olive oil.
  7. 7 Sprinkle everything bagel seasoning and garlic powder over salmon.
  8. 8 Add lemon slices on top of salmon.
  9. 9 Return to oven and bake for 12-15 minutes until salmon is cooked through.
  10. 10 Garnish with fresh dill and serve immediately.

Perfect Roasted Chicken

90 minutes
4 servings
Medium

Ingredients

  • ✓ 1 whole chicken (3-4 lbs)
  • ✓ 4 tablespoons butter, softened
  • ✓ 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • ✓ 2 tablespoons fresh herbs (rosemary, thyme, parsley)
  • ✓ 1 lemon, halved
  • ✓ 1 onion, quartered
  • ✓ 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • ✓ 1 tablespoon paprika
  • ✓ Salt and black pepper to taste
  • ✓ 1 cup chicken broth

Instructions

  1. 1 Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. 2 Pat chicken dry with paper towels.
  3. 3 Mix butter, garlic, and herbs together.
  4. 4 Gently separate chicken skin from meat and spread herb butter underneath.
  5. 5 Stuff cavity with lemon halves and onion quarters.
  6. 6 Rub outside with olive oil, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  7. 7 Tie legs together with kitchen string.
  8. 8 Place chicken breast-side up in a roasting pan. Pour broth in the bottom.
  9. 9 Roast for 60-75 minutes, basting every 20 minutes.
  10. 10 Chicken is done when internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).
  11. 11 Let rest for 10-15 minutes before carving.

Baked Teriyaki Chicken

40 minutes
4 servings
Easy

Ingredients

  • ✓ 6 chicken thighs (boneless, skinless)
  • ✓ 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • ✓ 1/4 cup honey
  • ✓ 1/4 cup mirin (or rice wine)
  • ✓ 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • ✓ 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • ✓ 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, grated
  • ✓ 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • ✓ 2 tablespoons water
  • ✓ Sesame seeds for garnish
  • ✓ Green onions, sliced for garnish

Instructions

  1. 1 Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. 2 In a bowl, mix soy sauce, honey, mirin, brown sugar, garlic, and ginger.
  3. 3 Reserve 1/4 cup of sauce for later. Pour rest over chicken and marinate for 30 minutes.
  4. 4 Place chicken in a baking dish and pour marinade over it.
  5. 5 Bake for 25-30 minutes, turning halfway through.
  6. 6 Meanwhile, mix cornstarch with water to make a slurry.
  7. 7 Remove chicken from oven and place on a serving plate.
  8. 8 Pour pan juices into a saucepan, add reserved sauce and cornstarch slurry.
  9. 9 Simmer until sauce thickens (2-3 minutes).
  10. 10 Pour sauce over chicken, garnish with sesame seeds and green onions.
  11. 11 Serve with steamed rice and vegetables.

Chicken Parmesan

35 minutes
4 servings
Medium

Ingredients

  • ✓ 4 chicken breasts, pounded thin
  • ✓ 2 cups marinara sauce
  • ✓ 1 1/2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • ✓ 1/2 cup parmesan cheese, grated
  • ✓ 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • ✓ 2 eggs, beaten
  • ✓ 2 cups Italian breadcrumbs
  • ✓ 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • ✓ 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • ✓ Salt and pepper to taste
  • ✓ Fresh basil for garnish
  • ✓ Cooked spaghetti for serving

Instructions

  1. 1 Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. 2 Season chicken with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning.
  3. 3 Set up breading station: flour in one dish, beaten eggs in another, breadcrumbs in third.
  4. 4 Coat each chicken breast in flour, then egg, then breadcrumbs.
  5. 5 Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  6. 6 Fry chicken for 3-4 minutes per side until golden brown.
  7. 7 Spread 1 cup marinara sauce in a baking dish.
  8. 8 Place fried chicken on top of sauce.
  9. 9 Spoon remaining marinara over each chicken breast.
  10. 10 Top with mozzarella and parmesan cheese.
  11. 11 Bake for 15-20 minutes until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  12. 12 Garnish with fresh basil and serve over spaghetti.
